# When We Grumble <br/> Against God
## Num14:1-10

---
## When We Grumble [(Num 14)](# "ref")

1. The Promised Land
1. Murmurs
1. The Severity of God
1. The Kindness of God

<!-- .element: class="r-fit-text" -->

---
I have surely seen the **affliction** <br/>
of my people who are in Egypt and have <br/>
heard their **cry** because of their taskmasters. 

I know their **sufferings**, and I have come down <br/>
to **deliver** them [...]  and to bring them [...]<br/>
to a **good and broad land**, <br/>
a land flowing with milk and honey

[Exodus 3:7-8 (ESV)](# "ref")

---
All the people of Israel <br/>
**grumbled** against Moses and Aaron.

"I have heard the **grumblings** <br/>
of the people of Israel, <br/>
which they grumble **against me**."

[Numbers 14:2,27 (ESV)](# "ref")

---
Would that we had **died** in the land of **Egypt**! <br/>
Or would that we had died in this **wilderness**! <br/>
Why is the Lord bringing us into this land, <br/>
to fall by the **sword**? 

Our wives and our little ones will <br/>
become a **prey**. Would it not be better <br/>
for us to **go back** to Egypt?”

[Numbers 14:2-3 (ESV)](# "ref")

---
Yet you [...] **rebelled** against <br/>
the command of the Lord your God. <br/>
And you **murmured** in your tents and said, 

"Because the Lord **hated** us he has <br/>
brought us out of the land of Egypt, <br/>
to give us into the hand of the Amorites, <br/>
to **destroy** us."

[Deuteronomy 1:26-27 (ESV)](# "ref")

---
The **kindness** and the **severity** of God: <br/>
severity toward those who have **fallen**, <br/>
but God's kindness to you, <br/>
provided you **continue** in his kindness. 

Otherwise you too will be **cut off**. <br/>
And even they, if they do not continue <br/>
in their unbelief, will be **grafted** in, <br/>
for God has the power to graft them in again.

[Romans 11:22-23 (ESV)](# "ref")
